Egg Salad

Descripton

Egg salad is an extremely simple to make, and delicious recipe to enjoy. Using chopped hard-boiled eggs, mayonaise, and mustard.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: Wouldn't be much of an egg salad without the eggs!
  • Condiments: You will need mayonaise and mustard.
  • Green onion: Green onions add great flavor and crunch!
  • Seasonings: You will need salt, pepper, and paprika.

Steps:

  1. Boil, peel, and chop the eggs.
  2. Combine the chopped eggs and the remaining ingredients.
  3. Serve on bread or with crackers.
